# MSI 2026 — Sources

Data collected 2026-07-04 for the bracket predictor artifact.

## Format
- 11 teams: top 2 from LCK, LPL, LEC, LCS, LCP + CBLOL champion (1 team).
- Play-In: 4 teams (T1, Karmine Corp, Team Liquid Alienware, Relove Deep Cross Gaming), double elimination, Bo5, June 28 – July 1. One team advances.
- Bracket Stage: 8 teams, double elimination, Bo5, Fearless Draft, July 3–12, Daejeon, South Korea.
  - Upper Bracket Round 1 → Round 2 (semis) → Upper Final
  - Lower Bracket Round 1 → Round 2 → Round 3 (semis) → Lower Final
  - Grand Final: July 12

## Play-In results (all 3-0 sweeps)
- R1: T1 def. Team Liquid Alienware 3-0
- R1: Karmine Corp def. Relove Deep Cross Gaming 3-0
- Upper Final: T1 def. Karmine Corp 3-0
- Lower R1: Team Liquid def. Deep Cross Gaming 3-0
- Lower Final: Team Liquid def. Karmine Corp 3-0
- Grand Final: T1 def. Team Liquid 3-0 → T1 advances to Bracket Stage (9-0 games)
